Jan Oblak

The Wall

Goalkeeper | 2014-Present

Biography

Jan Oblak is widely regarded as one of the best goalkeepers in the world and a cornerstone of Atletico Madrid's success. Since joining in 2014, he has established himself as a world-class shot-stopper with incredible reflexes, positioning, and leadership. He has won multiple Zamora Trophies (award for La Liga's best goalkeeper) and has been instrumental in Atletico's defensive excellence. His consistent performances and crucial saves have made him a legend at the club.

Achievements

  • La Liga Winner (2020-21)
  • UEFA Europa League Winner (2017-18)
  • UEFA Super Cup Winner (2018)
  • Champions League Finalist (2016)
  • Zamora Trophy Winner (2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19, 2020-21)
  • 400+ appearances for Atletico Madrid
  • One of the most decorated goalkeepers in La Liga history

Memorable Moments

  • Joined Atletico Madrid in 2014 from Benfica
  • Won first Zamora Trophy in 2015-16 season
  • Record-breaking five Zamora Trophies
  • Crucial saves in Champions League and La Liga campaigns
  • Became one of the most reliable goalkeepers in world football
