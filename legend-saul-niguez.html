Saúl Ñíguez

El Guerrero

Midfielder | 2012-2024

Biography

Saúl Ñíguez is one of the most complete and influential midfielders in Atletico Madrid's modern history. Known as "El Guerrero" (The Warrior), he is celebrated for his incredible versatility, tactical intelligence, and ability to excel in multiple positions. His work rate, defensive contributions, and attacking prowess made him a fan favorite and a crucial player in Atletico's success.

Achievements

  • La Liga Winner (2013-14)
  • Copa del Rey Winner (2012-13)
  • UEFA Europa League Winner (2011-12, 2017-18)
  • UEFA Super Cup Winner (2012, 2018)
  • Champions League Finalist (2014, 2016)
  • 350+ appearances for Atletico Madrid

Memorable Moments

  • Made his debut at age 17
  • Scored incredible bicycle kick in Champions League
  • Played in multiple positions with excellence
  • Became a symbol of Atletico's fighting spirit
