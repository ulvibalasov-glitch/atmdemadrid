LEGENDS

Honoring the legendary players who have shaped Atletico Madrid's history

Fernando Torres

Forward 2001-2007, 2015-2018

El Niño - One of Atletico's greatest strikers and beloved captain. Fernando Torres emerged from the club's youth academy to become a true icon, scoring 91 goals in his first spell. His explosive pace, clinical finishing, and unwavering passion made him a fan favorite.

175 appearances, 91 goals

Jan Oblak

Goalkeeper 2014-Present

One of the world's best goalkeepers and a pillar of Atletico's defense. Jan Oblak has established himself as an elite shot-stopper with incredible reflexes, positioning, and leadership qualities. His consistency and ability to make crucial saves in high-pressure situations have earned him multiple Zamora Trophies.

400+ appearances, Multiple Zamora Trophies

Learn More

Diego Godín

Defender 2010-2019

El Faraón - Iconic center-back and defensive leader who became a symbol of Atletico's defensive excellence. Diego Godín was the heart and soul of Atletico's legendary defense, known for his aerial prowess, tactical intelligence, and leadership.

389 appearances, 27 goals, Multiple titles

Learn More

Antoine Griezmann

Forward 2014-2019, 2021-Present

World Cup winner who became Atletico's all-time top scorer. Antoine Griezmann is a complete forward known for his exceptional skill, work rate, and passion for the game. His versatility allows him to play across the front line, creating chances and scoring spectacular goals.

300+ appearances, 150+ goals

Learn More

Koke

Midfielder 2009-Present

Current captain and one-club man who represents the heart and soul of modern Atletico Madrid. Koke has spent his entire career at the club, rising through the youth ranks to become captain and a true symbol of loyalty.

600+ appearances, Club legend

Rodrigo de Paul

Midfielder 2021-Present

El Argentino - Dynamic and creative midfielder who brings energy, vision, and technical excellence to Atletico's midfield. Rodrigo de Paul is known for his exceptional passing range, work rate, and ability to create opportunities while maintaining defensive discipline.

100+ appearances, 15+ goals, 25+ assists

Learn More

Saúl Ñíguez

Midfielder 2012-2024

El Guerrero - Versatile midfield dynamo who became one of the most complete and influential players in Atletico's modern history. Saúl Ñíguez was known for his incredible work rate, tactical intelligence, and ability to play multiple positions flawlessly.

350+ appearances, 50+ goals, 40+ assists, Multiple titles

Radamel Falcao

Forward 2011-2013

El Tigre - Prolific striker who scored 70 goals in 91 appearances, including a spectacular Europa League triumph. Radamel Falcao was a goal-scoring machine whose clinical finishing and aerial ability made him one of the most feared strikers in Europe.

91 appearances, 70 goals
