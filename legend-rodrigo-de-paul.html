Rodrigo de Paul

El Argentino

Midfielder | 2021-Present

Biography

Rodrigo de Paul is a dynamic and creative midfielder who has become a key player in Atletico Madrid's modern success. Known for his exceptional passing range, work rate, and technical ability, he brings energy and creativity to the midfield. His ability to create opportunities while maintaining defensive discipline makes him a perfect fit for Diego Simeone's system.

Achievements

  • World Cup Winner (2022)
  • Copa América Winner (2021)
  • La Liga Winner (2020-21)
  • Key player in Atletico's midfield

Memorable Moments
