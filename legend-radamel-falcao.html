Radamel Falcao

El Tigre

Forward | 2011-2013

Biography

Radamel Falcao, known as "El Tigre" (The Tiger), is one of the most prolific strikers in Atletico Madrid's history. In just two seasons, he scored 70 goals in 91 appearances, including a record-breaking performance in the Europa League. His clinical finishing, aerial ability, and goal-scoring instinct made him one of the most feared strikers in Europe.

Achievements

  • UEFA Europa League Winner (2011-12)
  • UEFA Super Cup Winner (2012)
  • Europa League Top Scorer (2011-12)
  • Scored 70 goals in 91 appearances

Memorable Moments

  • Scored hat-trick in Europa League final (2012)
  • Record-breaking goal-scoring season
  • Became one of Europe's most sought-after strikers
  • Left a lasting legacy despite short stay
