Antoine Griezmann

Grizou

Forward | 2014-2019, 2021-Present

Biography

Antoine Griezmann is Atletico Madrid's all-time top scorer and one of the most talented forwards in the club's history. After joining from Real Sociedad in 2014, he quickly became a fan favorite with his skill, creativity, and passion. He won the World Cup with France in 2018 and has been instrumental in Atletico's success, scoring crucial goals and creating countless memorable moments.

Achievements

  • Atletico Madrid All-Time Top Scorer
  • World Cup Winner (2018)
  • UEFA Europa League Winner (2017-18)
  • UEFA Super Cup Winner (2018)
  • La Liga Winner (2020-21)
  • European Championship Runner-up (2016)
  • Ballon d'Or Third Place (2016, 2018)

Memorable Moments

  • Became Atletico's all-time top scorer
  • Scored crucial goals in Europa League triumph
  • Won World Cup with France in 2018
  • Returned to Atletico in 2021 after spell at Barcelona
